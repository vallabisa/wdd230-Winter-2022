Target Audience

The target audience of this website are business professionals in Singapore and the surrounding region.

Personas

The first persona are business owners. This also includes CEO's, CFO's and Directors of the company.

The second persona are business professionals belonging to any business entity. This includes managers, marketers, sales officers and any business professional.

Scenarios

1. Why is Singapore Chamber of Commerce important to me?

2. What does Singapore Chamber of Commerce do?

3. How can Singapore Chamber of Commerce help me improve my business?

4. How to connect with succesful businesses in the region?

5. How to expand my business?
